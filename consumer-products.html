The new frontier of efficiency is about sustainability

While waste reduction continues to be a top focus for continuous improvement, further gains to efficiency require the addition of sensors to capture micro-losses as well as digitalization of paper-based processes to reduce information-related waste.

While losses are captured as part of Overall Equipment Efficiency (or Overall Labor Efficiency), this index also needs to include losses tied to sustainability metrics (water, air, gas, electricity, steam) as it is a driver of cost efficiency as well as brand equity.

Attracting and retaining a quality workforce is a top priority for manufacturers

Faced with a scarcity of skilled workers, manufacturers are responding by boosting recruitment, adopting new technologies and taking steps to maximize the productivity and experience of their older workers.
